Introduction

Rev. Fr. Moses Orshio Adasu University formerly known as BSU Consultancy was established by Governing Council of the University at the inception of University under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of the Federal Republic

Rev. Fr. Moses Orshio Adasu University Consultancy Services is dedicated to empowering individual, organizations, and institutions with the essential skills and knowledge needed to build a sustainable future. We achieve this through financial education, tailored training programs, and professional advisory services.

The Consultancy Unit is highly valued for its competence in market research, human resources development, project management and policy development. We design and deliver programs which ensure that our clients have the skills and knowledge to impact positively in their organization, access financial services and promote social inclusion.

The Consultancy Services is prepared to successfully deliver a variety of training programs, including CEFTER, Food Station, Management, Leadership Development, Programming, Computer Literacy, Fundamentals of administration and more.
